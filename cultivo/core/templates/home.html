Facts

Farming is a huge boon to our economy that means it is one of the major sectors that influences a country’s economic growth. In India, farmers are considered to be the backbone of the country. Around 52% of the Indian population account to farmers having agriculture as the sole occupation. Unfortunately, today’s agriculture policies fail to recognize how crop choices, input costs. India needs to shift from basic farming to more efficient, sustainable, and productive farming.

Solution

In order to mitigate the agrarian crisis in the current status quo, there is a need for better recommendation systems to alleviate the crisis by helping the farmers to make an informed decision before starting the cultivation of crops. It is essential to the success of the society as a whole, without farming everyone would be forced to hunt and gather food, and it is not possible for the world’s population to survive off such an eating style.

Our Idea

It's just a small initiative. Our main aim is to recommend optimum crops to be cultivated by farmers based on several parameters and help them make an informed decision before cultivation. It's an interactive platform where the farmers can communicate as it is easily understandable. It a start of building all in one solution for the farmers.Through this platform relatively less experienced farmers can solve their queries, doubts and questions also.
